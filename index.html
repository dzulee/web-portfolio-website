Web Designer , Data analyst & project manager

As a web designer, I harness the power of HTML (Hyper Text Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheet), JavaScript (JS), and PHP to craft visually appealing designs and imbue tab links with functionality. The portfolio showcased here is a testament to my craftsmanship and expertise in this field.


A hallmark of my work is the creation of compelling and narrative-driven dashboards, showcasing my prowess in data analysis. Leveraging tools like Power BI, I adeptly distill insights from raw data, illuminating key performance indicators (KPIs) and offering valuable insights.


Python serves as my go-to language for data analysis, facilitated by platforms like Jupyter Notebook. With libraries such as pandas, numpy, and matplotlib, I engage in data wrangling and provide comprehensive explanations of datasets. SQL forms the bedrock of my analytical journey, enabling efficient data management and analysis. My projects are a reflection of my continuous learning journey and serve as milestones in my professional growth. I eagerly await the opportunity to collaborate on future projects and welcome any inquiries.

My Story

I am deeply passionate about the realms of web design, graphic design, data analysis, and project management. My journey within the tech sphere, though not devoid of challenges as many would attest, has been rich with learning experiences and moments of admiration. It all began with my exploration into the world of data analysis, where Microsoft Power BI swiftly became my tool of choice for its analytical prowess. Expanding my horizons, I delved into Tableau, adding another valuable skillset to my repertoire.
Guided by trainings like power bi week, I honed my abilities in Power BI, mastering the art of dashboarding and weaving insightful story telling dashboards from raw numerical data, measures and adept at unraveling diverse analytical queries.
Further enriching my proficiency, I embarked on a journey of self-improvement in Python programming. This endeavor has proven to be a transformative force, empowering me to manipulate data fluidly and subject it to a spectrum of analytical models. From linear regression to logistic regression and Support Vector Machines (SVM), my grasp of Python has equipped me with formidable tools for data exploration and predictive analysis.

My academic journey culminated in September 2022, with the attainment of a degree in Information Technology, a pursuit that commenced in 2017 at Taita Taveta University. Along this path, I've come to deeply value the essence of teamwork, recognizing that collaborative efforts fuel exponential growth. In the crucible of teamwork, energy, camaraderie, and invaluable guidance intertwine, fostering an environment where skills flourish and innovation thrives.
